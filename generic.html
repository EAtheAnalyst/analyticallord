Introduction to Data Analysis

In this class, I taught the students fundamentals of data analysis i.e the building block of data analysis [Good business knowledge, Good methodology, Good data]. I started with types of data analysis, different methodical approaches to analysis, different approaches to problem-solving [Root cause analysis, Gap analysis, Blindspot analysis], I also taught how to develop framework to solve any problem using a real business case.
